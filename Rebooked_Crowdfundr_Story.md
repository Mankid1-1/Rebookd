## What Rebooked Does

Rebooked gives your business **19 pre-built SMS automations** that run silently in the background, 24/7. Here's what that looks like in practice:

**When a client no-shows** → They get a friendly follow-up text within the hour, with an easy link to rebook. Most people just forgot. A gentle nudge works.

**When someone cancels** → Rebooked sends an acknowledgement, then a rebook offer 24 hours later. Meanwhile, it texts your waiting list so that slot fills immediately.

**After a visit** → Clients get a feedback request, then an upsell offer. Happy clients get automatically prompted to leave a review.

**When a client goes quiet** → Rebooked sends a 30-day win-back, then a 90-day win-back. A simple "we miss you" text with a booking link recovers more clients than you'd expect.

**For new leads** → A welcome message fires instantly, followed by a 3-day and 7-day follow-up. No lead falls through the cracks.

And that's just the beginning. Birthday promos, loyalty milestones, calendar sync, automated rescheduling, custom message templates — it's all built in.

**The pricing is straightforward:** $199 per month, plus 15% of the revenue Rebooked recovers for you. That means I only make money when you do. If it's not delivering for you, you're not paying extra.
