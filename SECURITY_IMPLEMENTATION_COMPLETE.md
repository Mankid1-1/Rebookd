## 🎯 Key Features Implemented

### **Data Protection & Privacy**
- ✅ **Encryption**: All PII encrypted at rest and in transit
- ✅ **Data Minimization**: Only collect necessary data fields
- ✅ **Retention Policies**: Automatic cleanup (7 years user, 5 years leads, 3 years messages)
- ✅ **Right to Erasure**: Soft-delete with permanent cleanup
- ✅ **Consent Management**: Explicit consent recording with timestamps

### **Communications Compliance**
- ✅ **TCPA Compliance**: Prior express consent required for SMS marketing
- ✅ **Consent Recording**: Timestamp, source, language, evidence stored
- ✅ **STOP Compliance**: 5-minute response to opt-outs
- ✅ **Frequency Limits**: 1/day, 3/week, 8/month marketing SMS
- ✅ **Quiet Hours**: 9PM-9AM restrictions enforced
- ✅ **CAN-SPAM**: Email marketing compliance with unsubscribe links

### **Authentication & Access Control**
- ✅ **Password Security**: 12+ chars with complexity (NIST 800-63B)
- ✅ **Session Management**: 8-hour max, 30-minute inactivity timeout
- ✅ **Multi-Factor Authentication**: Required for admin actions
- ✅ **Rate Limiting**: 5 attempts/15min auth, 1000 requests/min API
- ✅ **Secure Sessions**: HttpOnly, Secure, SameSite=Strict cookies

### **Security Monitoring**
- ✅ **Real-time Threat Detection**: Anomaly detection and automated response
- ✅ **Comprehensive Audit Trail**: All security events logged
- ✅ **Incident Response**: 15-minute critical response time
- ✅ **SIEM-like Monitoring**: Continuous security surveillance
