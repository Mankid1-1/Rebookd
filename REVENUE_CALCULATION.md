---

## 🧮 **Step-by-Step Calculation**

### **Per Client Revenue**
```
Monthly Appointments: 40
Appointment Value: $60
Total Monthly Revenue: 40 × $60 = $2,400

Revenue Share (15%): $2,400 × 0.15 = $360
Base Subscription: $199
Total Monthly Bill: $199 + $360 = $559
```

### **All 3 Clients Combined**
```
Total Monthly Revenue: 3 × $2,400 = $7,200
Total Revenue Share: 3 × $360 = $1,080
Total Base Fees: 3 × $199 = $597
Total Monthly Income: $1,080 + $597 = $1,677
```
